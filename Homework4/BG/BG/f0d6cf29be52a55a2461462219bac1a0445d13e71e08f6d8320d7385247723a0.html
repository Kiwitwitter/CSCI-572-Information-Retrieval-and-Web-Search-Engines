Standout high school athletes highlighted by The Boston Globe.

10 moments to remember from high school sports this spring

The spring season produced magical moments and stirring finishes. Here are 10 that made us take notice. 

Support real journalism. Subscribe to The Boston Globe.

2017 Softball All-Scholastics

Milford’s Ali Atherton, Norton’s Kelly Nelson, and Austin Prep’s Logan MacDonald are the Players of the Year. 

2017 Baseball All-Scholastics

Mike Vasil, Henry Funaro, Tylor Arruda, Ryan Sliwinski, and Casey Earle were named Players of the Year. 

2017 Para Athlete All-Scholastics

Mikayla Chandler, Cailin Currie, Chris Kimball, Matt Freitas, and Elise Morley earned All-Scholastics honors. 

2017 Boys’ Lacrosse All-Scholastics

Hayden Frey, Frankie Higgins, and Kilian Morrissey earned Player of the Year honors. 

2017 Girls’ Lacrosse All-Scholastics

Waltham’s Taylor Pinzone and Cohasset’s Elle Hansen earned Player of the Year honors. 

2017 Girls’ Golf All-Scholastics

Concord-Carlisle’s Gabrielle Shieh was named Golfer of the Year. 

2017 Boys’ Volleyball All-Scholastics

Wayland’s Jack Fletcher earned Player of the Year honors. 

2017 Girls’ Outdoor Track & Field All-Scholastics

Clare Martin of Newton South was named Athlete of the Year. 

2017 Boys’ Outdoor Track & Field All-Scholastics

North Reading’s Erick Duffy was named the Athlete of the Year. 

2017 Rugby All-Scholastics

Hanover’s Jeffrey Wheeler and Coleman Lambert and BC High’s Chris Troiano and Ryan Connolly are among the honorees. 

2017 Boys’ Tennis All-Scholastics

Dover-Sherborn’s Max Schuermann was named the Player of the Year. 

2017 Girls’ Tennis All-Scholastics

Mia Wallace of Arlington was named Player of the Year.
