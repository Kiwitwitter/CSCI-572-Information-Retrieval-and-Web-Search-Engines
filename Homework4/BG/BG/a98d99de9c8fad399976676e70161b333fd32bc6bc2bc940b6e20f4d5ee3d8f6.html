GlobeWest: Noteworthy performances

Luke Tracy

Southborough’s Jacob Maddox made his first collegiate soccer goal for Franklin Pierce a big one.

By Logan Mullen Globe Correspondent  September 22, 2017

EMILY CARENS

NORTHBOROUGH

The junior outside hitter for the Worcester State volleyball team was named the Massachusetts State Collegiate Athletic Conference Player of the Week after averaging 12 kills, five digs, and five blocks per game in a 2-1 week.

JACOB MADDOX

Advertisement

SOUTHBOROUGH

The sophomore midfielder for Franklin Pierce’s mens’ soccer squad scored his first collegiate goal at a big moment, finding the back of the net in the 102d minute of sudden-death overtime Sept. 12, securing a 1-0 win over Le Moyne.

KATHRYN SALEMME

BOXBOROUGH

The senior keeper for Worcester State field hockey was named the Little East Conference Goalkeeper of the Week after a 2-0 week. She made four saves in a 2-1 win over Salve Regina, and 10 saves in a win over Keene State.
