New York Jets vs. Miami

Time: 9:30 a.m., CBS. Line: Jets by 1½.

Records: Jets 2-1 (2-1 vs. spread), Dolphins 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread).

Notes: Game is in London . . . Todd Bowles, in first year as Jets coach, was interim coach for Dolphins for last three games of 2011 . . . Jets have lost 8 yards in sacks, fewest in AFC, and Dolphins have one sack in 94 pass plays, worst in NFL . . . Jets have allowed league-low 41 points . . . D olphins have been outscored, 27-3, in first quarter . . . Miami is one of three teams with no TDs rushing.

Key injuries: JETS: OUT: G Willie Colon (knee), TE Jeff Cumberland (concussion), WR Chris Owusu (knee), LB Trevor Reilly (finger), CB Darrin Walls (hamstring). QUESTIONABLE: WR Eric Decker (knee). Barnes (ankle), DE Stephen Bowen (knee), G James Carpenter (back), LB Quinton Coples (elbow), CB Antonio Cromartie (hip), LB David Harris (finger), RB Chris Ivory (quadriceps), S Jaiquawn Jarrett (knee), CB Darrelle Revis (hamstring), WR Devin Smith (ribs). DOLPHINS: OUT: TE Dion Sims (concussion). DOUBTFUL: T Branden Albert (hamstring). QUESTIONABLE: TE Jordan Cameron (groin), RB Jonas Gray (calf). PROBABLE: DT Earl Mitchell (back).

Carolina at Tampa Bay

Time: 1 p.m. Line: Panthers by 3.

Records: Panthers 3-0 (3-0 vs. spread), Buccaneers 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread).

Notes: QBs Cam Newton and Jameis Winston are Heisman Trophy winners who won national championships in college and were selected No. 1 overall in NFL Draft . . . Panthers All-Pro LB Luke Kuechly hasn’t played since leaving opener against Jacksonville with concussion . . . Carolina acquired DE Jared Allen from Chicago in trade this week . . . Bucs have yet to win at home under coach Lovie Smith (0-9).

Key injuries: PANTHERS: OUT: WR Jerricho Cotchery (ankle), LB Luke Kuechly (concussion), G Amini Silatolu (ankle), T Daryl Williams (knee). PROBABLE: WR Corey Brown (illness), LB Thomas Davis (chest), DT Dwan Edwards (not injury related), CB Josh Norman (not injury related), RB Jonathan Stewart (tibia), CB Charles Tillman (not injury related), RB Mike Tolbert (groin). BUCCANEERS: DOUBTFUL: CB Johnthan Banks (knee), TE Austin Seferian-Jenkins (shoulder), C Evan Smith (ankle), TE Luke Stocker (hip). QUESTIONABLE: DE George Johnson (neck), RB Doug Martin (knee, quadriceps), DT Gerald McCoy (shoulder), WR Russell Shepard (hamstring), T Donovan Smith (knee), S Major Wright (abdomen). PROBABLE: S Chris Conte (hip).

Jacksonville at Indianapolis

Time: 1 p.m. Line: None.

Records: Jaguars 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread), Colts 1-2 (0-3 vs. spread).

Notes: Colts QB Andrew Luck (sore right shoulder) is expected to play despite missing practice time this week . . . Jaguars have lost five straight in series . . . Jacksonville has lost four straight road games vs. AFC South . . . Jaguars and Buccaneers are tied for third-lowest points total in NFL (49) . . . WR Allen Robinson is second in NFL in yards per reception (22.7) . . . QB Blake Bortles has better passer rating (78.3) than Luck (65.1).

Key injuries: JAGUARS: OUT: WR Marqise Lee (hamstring), G Brandon Linder (shoulder), DT Sen'Derrick Marks (knee), RB Denard Robinson (knee), TE Julius Thomas (hand). DOUBTFUL: S Sergio Brown (calf). PROBABLE: G Zane Beadles (finger), DE Chris Clemons (not injury related), S Josh Evans (knee), CB Davon House (lower leg), WR Allen Hurns (thigh), DT Roy Miller III (knee), G Tyler Shatley (thumb). COLTS: OUT: CB Greg Toler (neck), RB Tyler Varga (concussion). QUESTIONABLE: TE Dwayne Allen (ankle), QB Andrew Luck (shoulder). PROBABLE: CB Darius Butler (hip), LB Trent Cole (knee), CB Vontae Davis (ankle), WR T.Y. Hilton (knee), RB Josh Robinson (back), G Hugh Thornton (knee).

New York Giants at Buffalo

Time: 1 p.m., Fox. Line: Bills by 5½.

Records: Giants 1-2 (2-1 vs. spread), Bills 2-1 (2-1 vs. spread).

Notes:  Giants have been outscored, 43-24, in final 15 minutes, and squandered double-digit fourth-quarter leads in both losses . . . RB LeSean McCoy (hamstring) is out, giving rookie Karlos Williams his first career start . . . Bills have topped 100 yards rushing in three straight for first time since final three games of 2013.

Key injuries: GIANTS: OUT: DE Robert Ayers Jr. (hamstring), WR Victor Cruz (calf), TE Jerome Cunningham (knee), DT Markus Kuhn (knee). QUESTIONABLE: TE Daniel Fells (ankle), T Ereck Flowers (ankle). PROBABLE: RB Orleans Darkwa (knee), DE Owamagbe Odighizuwa (foot). BILLS: OUT: WR Marquise Goodwin (ribs), RB LeSean McCoy (hamstring), G John Miller (groin), WR Sammy Watkins (calf), S Aaron Williams (neck). PROBABLE: LB Preston Brown (hamstring), K Dan Carpenter (left knee), WR Percy Harvin (hip), QB Tyrod Taylor (ankle).

Kansas City at Cincinnati

Time: 1 p.m., CBS. Line: Bengals by 3½.

Records: Chiefs 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread), Bengals 3-0 (3-0 vs. spread).

Notes:  Chiefs haven’t started 1-3 since 2012 . . . Chiefs have allowed an NFL-high 14 sacks . . . Win would match Bengals’ third-best start in club history; the 1975 and 1988 teams opened 6-0 . . . QB Andy Dalton’s 121 passer rating is second to Aaron Rodgers (135.4).

Key injuries: CHIEFS: QUESTIONABLE: WR Albert Wilson (shoulder). PROBABLE: WR Jason Avant (knee), WR Chris Conley (shoulder), CB Jamell Fleming (hip), LB Josh Mauga (Achilles). BENGALS: DOUBTFUL: S George Iloka (ankle). QUESTIONABLE: DT Marcus Hardison (knee), CB Adam Jones (elbow). PROBABLE: CB Dre Kirkpatrick (shoulder), DT Pat Sims (hip).

Oakland at Chicago

Time: 1 p.m. Line: Raiders by 3.

Records: Raiders 2-1 (2-1 vs. spread), Bears 0-3 (0-3 vs. spread).

Notes: Raiders have won two straight after season-opening loss to Cincinnati . . . WR Amari Cooper leads NFL rookies in catches (20) and yards receiving (290) . . . Chicago got shut out for first time since 2002 last week.

Key injuries: RAIDERS: OUT: DE Benson Mayowa (knee), CB Keith McGill (foot), DT C.J. Wilson (calf). QUESTIONABLE: LB Ben Heeney (hamstring), RB Taiwan Jones (foot), S Charles Woodson (shoulder). PROBABLE: DT Justin Ellis (ankle), DE Khalil Mack (hip), RB Jamize Olawale (ankle), DE Justin Tuck (knee). BEARS: OUT: T Jermon Bushrod (concussion, shoulder). QUESTIONABLE: CB Alan Ball (groin), QB Jay Cutler (hamstring), DT Ego Ferguson (knee), WR Alshon Jeffery (hamstring), P Patrick O'Donnell (right knee), DT Jeremiah Ratliff (ankle), DT Will Sutton (elbow).

Houston at Atlanta

Time: 1 p.m. Line: Falcons by 5½.

Records: Texans 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread), Falcons 3-0 (3-0 vs. spread).

Notes: RB Alfred Blue has 467 yards rushing in six career starts . . . RB Arian Foster could return from groin injury . . . QB Ryan Mallett improved to 2-2 as starter by passing for 228 yards, one TD in win over Buccaneers . . . Falcons are first NFL team to be 3-0 when trailing in fourth quarter of each game . . . Julio Jones’s 34 catches are most in NFL history through three games.

Key injuries: TEXANS: OUT: S Lonnie Ballentine (knee), LB Akeem Dent (hamstring), RB Jonathan Grimes (knee). QUESTIONABLE: T Duane Brown (hand), RB Arian Foster (groin). PROBABLE: G Brandon Brooks (ankle), LB Jadeveon Clowney (shoulder), CB Johnathan Joseph (hip), QB Ryan Mallett (chest), T Derek Newton (ankle), S Eddie Pleasant (thigh), G Xavier Su'a-Filo (calf). FALCONS: OUT: S Ricardo Allen (knee), RB Tevin Coleman (ribs), WR Devin Hester (toe), TE Jacob Tamme (concussion). PROBABLE: WR Julio Jones (toe, hamstring), LB Brooks Reed (groin).

Cleveland at San Diego

Time: 4:05 p.m. Line: Chargers by 7.

Records: Browns 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread), Chargers 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread).

Notes: Browns have lost three straight in San Diego . . . Chargers have only one sack in three games, while QB Philip Rivers has been sacked 10 times.

Key injuries: BROWNS: OUT: DE Desmond Bryant (shoulder), LB Craig Robertson (ankle), LB Scott Solomon (ankle), RB Robert Turbin (ankle), CB K'Waun Williams (concussion). QUESTIONABLE: RB Shaun Draughn (back), S Tashaun Gipson (groin). PROBABLE: WR Travis Benjamin (ribs), LB Karlos Dansby (ankle), CB Justin Gilbert (hamstring), CB Joe Haden (ribs, finger), QB Johnny Manziel (right elbow), QB Josh McCown (right hand), T Mitchell Schwartz (thumb). CHARGERS: OUT: T King Dunlap (concussion), G Orlando Franklin (ankle), WR Jacoby Jones (ankle), CB Craig Mager (hamstring), LB Tourek Williams (foot). DOUBTFUL: C Chris Watt (groin). QUESTIONABLE: S Jahleel Addae (ankle), T D.J. Fluker (ankle, chest), TE Ladarius Green (concussion), CB Jason Verrett (foot). PROBABLE: CB Brandon Flowers (knee), T Chris Hairston (ankle, knee).

Green Bay at San Francisco

Time: 4:25 p.m., Fox. Line: Packers by 7½.

Records: Packers 3-0 (3-0 vs. spread), 49ers 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread).

Notes: Fifth meeting between teams since 2012 . . . 49ers have won last four since Green Bay beat San Francisco, 34-16, Dec. 5, 2010, at Lambeau Field for eight-game winning streak in series. Three of last four 49ers’ wins decided by 8 or fewer points . . . In six starts vs. San Fran. including playoffs, Aaron Rodgers has 13 TDs, 3 interceptions, and 103.7 passer rating . . . 49ers gave up 90 points over two games for first time since 1980 . . . 49ers QB Colin Kaepernick threw four interceptions in 47-7 loss at Arizona last week, with two pick-6s.

Key injuries: PACKERS: OUT: T Bryan Bulaga (knee). DOUBTFUL: CB Demetri Goodson (hamstring), LB Jake Ryan (hamstring). QUESTIONABLE: WR Davante Adams (ankle), S Morgan Burnett (calf). PROBABLE: WR Randall Cobb (shoulder), DE Datone Jones (head), RB Eddie Lacy (ankle), LB Mike Neal (groin), S Sean Richardson (ankle), RB Aaron Ripkowski (shoulder). 49ERS: DOUBTFUL: TE Vernon Davis (knee). PROBABLE: CB Kenneth Acker (back), G Alex Boone (shoulder), LB Ahmad Brooks (shoulder), RB Reggie Bush (calf), WR Bruce Ellington (ankle), S L.J. McCray (hip), TE Vance McDonald (knee), S Eric Reid (hip).

St. Louis at Arizona

Time: 4:25 p.m. Line: Cardinals by 7.

Records: Rams 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread), Cardinals 3-0 (3-0 vs. spread).

Notes: Arizona’s Larry Fitzgerald has as many TDs (5) as entire Rams team . . . In first quarter, Rams have been outscored, 20-0, while Cardinals have outscored opponents, 35-10 . . . Arizona has 126 points through three games, Rams 50 . . . St. Louis has 13 sacks . . . Arizona’s Carson Palmer sacked once, fewest in NFL.

Key injuries: RAMS: OUT: S Maurice Alexander (groin), DE Eugene Sims (knee). DOUBTFUL: RB Chase Reynolds (knee). PROBABLE: WR Kenny Britt (shoulder), RB Benny Cunningham (knee). CARDINALS: OUT: WR J.J. Nelson (shoulder). QUESTIONABLE: RB Andre Ellington (knee). PROBABLE: WR John Brown (shoulder), G Mike Iupati (knee), LB Alex Okafor (shoulder), LB LaMarr Woodley (quadriceps).

Minnesota at Denver

Time: 4:25 p.m. Line: Broncos by 7.

Records: Vikings 2-1 (2-1 vs. spread), Broncos 3-0 (3-0 vs. spread).

Notes: Only game this weekend that pits two teams with winning records . . . QB Teddy Bridgewater has won three of last four . . . Broncos OLB DeMarcus Ware is only player in league with sack in each game this season . . . Peyton Manning is 3-0 vs. Minnesota.

Key injuries: VIKINGS: OUT: WR Charles Johnson (rib), CB Jabari Price (shoulder), S Andrew Sendejo (knee), DE Justin Trattou (foot). QUESTIONABLE: LB Audie Cole (ankle), WR Jarius Wright (hand). PROBABLE: CB Xavier Rhodes (concussion, neck). BRONCOS: OUT: T Ty Sambrailo (shoulder). QUESTIONABLE: TE James Casey (knee), LB Todd Davis (ankle). PROBABLE: DE Kenny Anunike (knee), S Omar Bolden (foot), TE Owen Daniels (not injury related), QB Peyton Manning (not injury related), G Evan Mathis (hamstring), RB Juwan Thompson (neck), G Louis Vasquez (knee), DE Vance Walker (elbow), CB Kayvon Webster (ankle).

Dallas at New Orleans

Time: 8:30 p.m., NBC. Line: Saints by 4½.

Records: Cowboys 2-1 (1-2 vs. spread), Saints 0-3 (0-3 vs. spread).

Notes: Cowboys can set club record for consecutive road victories with 11 . . . Saints are 0-3 for third time since 2007.

Key injuries: COWBOYS: OUT: WR Dez Bryant (foot), DE Randy Gregory (ankle). PROBABLE: CB Brandon Carr (not injury related), LB Andrew Gachkar (foot), G Ronald Leary (groin), DE Jeremy Mincey (concussion), DE Ryan Russell (groin), LB Kyle Wilber (hamstring), TE Jason Witten (ankle, knee). SAINTS: OUT: G Jahri Evans (knee). PROBABLE: QB Drew Brees (right shoulder), S Jairus Byrd (knee), WR Brandin Cooks (ankle), LB Dannell Ellerbe (toe), DE Cameron Jordan (back), CB Keenan Lewis (hip).

Detroit at Seattle

Time: Mon., 8:30 p.m., ESPN.

Line: Seahawks by 9½.

Records: Lions 0-3 (0-3 vs. spread), Seahawks 1-2 (1-2 vs. spread).

Notes: Lions trying to avoid first 0-4 start since 2010 . . . Seahawks are 13-2 in prime-time games under Pete Carroll. QB Matthew Stafford tied for second in league with 82 pass completions but has passer rating of 78.7 . . . Lions rank last in rushing at just 45 yards per game.

Key injuries: LIONS: DNP: RB Joique Bell (ankle), TE Brandon Pettigrew (hamstring). LIMITED: DE Ezekiel Ansah (groin), LB DeAndre Levy (hip), T Corey Robinson (ankle), CB Josh Wilson (lower leg).
