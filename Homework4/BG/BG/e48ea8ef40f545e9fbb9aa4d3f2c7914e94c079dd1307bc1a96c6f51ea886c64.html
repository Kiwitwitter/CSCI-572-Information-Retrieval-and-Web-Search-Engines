‘SNL’ returning soon with Gosling and Gadot

Frederick M. Brown/Getty Images

“Wonder Woman” Gal Gadot will host “Saturday Night Live” on Oct. 7.

By Matthew Gilbert Globe Staff  September 22, 2017

Last season, “Saturday Night Live” hit ratings highs with some memorable political satire, which led to some big Emmy wins last Sunday.

The show will be back next Saturday, Sept. 30, and I’m hoping the cast, along with regular guest Alec Baldwin as Donald Trump, will continue to shine.

Advertisement

Here’s the hosting news:

Ryan Gosling will head up the season 43 season premiere, with musical guest Jay-Z.

Gal Gadot of “Wonder Woman” fame will host the Oct. 7 episode, with musical guest Sam Smith.

And Kumail Nanjiani of “The Big Sick” and “Silicon Valley” will take over the Oct. 14 episode, with musical guest Pink.
