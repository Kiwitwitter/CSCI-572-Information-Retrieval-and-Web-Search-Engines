Imagining Trump tweets, unabridged

The Boston Globe Tweet Share

By Nestor Ramos Globe Staff  September 27, 2017

Maybe Twitter has been the problem all along.

Perhaps our nation’s leader has been stymied, again and again, by the constraints of the social media service’s 140 character limit. How are you supposed to fit the best words in 140 characters? What’s a character, even? Does a space count? (Fact check: yes). What about a picture? That’s like, a thousand words worth of characters, right? (Fact check: no).

Advertisement

It’s possible that Donald Trump, alone with his phone, has all along been tapping out thoughtful, statesmanlike missives to help the public interpret and comprehend what’s going on in the halls of power, only to be received as a raving gasbag when his carefully crafted thoughts appear cut in half. Now that Twitter is testing out doubling its character limit to 280, Trump could soon dig into his Twitter drafts folder and fire off the nuanced views for which he will become known.

Kim Jong Un of North Korea, who is obviously a madman who doesn't mind starving or killing his people, will be tested like never before!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 22, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

Kim Jong Un of North Korea, who is obviously a madman who doesn’t mind starving or killing his people, will be tested like never before! Through strategic sanctions and careful diplomacy, of course! Madman is term of endearment in parts of Europe and Asia, FYI.

Ratings for NFL football are way down except before game starts, when people tune in to see whether or not our country will be disrespected!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 26, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

Ratings for NFL football are way down except before game starts, when people tune in to see whether or not our country will be disrespected! It wasn’t: The right to peacefully protest against inequality is fundamental to our way of life, and critical to our growth as a nation!

The booing at the NFL football game last night, when the entire Dallas team dropped to its knees, was loudest I have ever heard. Great anger

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 26, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

The booing at the NFL football game last night, when the entire Dallas team dropped to its knees, was loudest I have ever heard. Great anger, which is odd, because who would both be deeply offended by supposed disrespect for the flag but then boo at it? Makes no sense.

Loser terrorists must be dealt with in a much tougher manner.The internet is their main recruitment tool which we must cut off & use better!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 15, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

Loser terrorists must be dealt with in a much tougher manner.The internet is their main recruitment tool which we must cut off & use better! Especially Twitter. Needs (a) more characters and (b) fewer Nazis and lonely men with frog avatars.

The travel ban into the United States should be far larger, tougher and more specific-but stupidly, that would not be politically correct!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 15, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

The travel ban into the United States should be far larger, tougher and more specific-but stupidly, that would not be politically correct! No, the important thing is that it would not be morally correct -- as everyone knows, we are nation of immigrants!

The greatest influence over our election was the Fake News Media "screaming" for Crooked Hillary Clinton. Next, she was a bad candidate!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 22, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

The greatest influence over our election was the Fake News Media “screaming” for Crooked Hillary Clinton. Next, she was a bad candidate! But I always thought they made too much of the email thing. GMail, so easy. Ever tried WhatsApp? Big overseas. Jared loves it.

ESPN is paying a really big price for its politics (and bad programming). People are dumping it in RECORD numbers. Apologize for untruth!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 15, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

ESPN is paying a really big price for its politics (and bad programming). People are dumping it in RECORD numbers. Apologize for untruth! Oops, typo. I meant I APOLOGIZE. Nobody is dumping ESPN, they’re cutting cable. Try calling Comcast to cancel ESPN, it’s not even a thing.

Fascinating to watch people writing books and major articles about me and yet they know nothing about me & have zero access. #FAKE NEWS!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 12, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

Fascinating to watch people writing books and major articles about me and yet they know nothing about me & have zero access. #FAKE NEWS! That’s why I’ve agreed to several sit-downs with major news outlets, no strings attached -- eager to talk policy! Quiz me!

Just got back to the White House from the Great States of Texas and Louisiana, where things are going well. Such cooperation & coordination!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 3, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

Just got back to the White House from the Great States of Texas and Louisiana, where things are going well. Such cooperation & coordination! And while no one knows whether one storm was the result of climate change, scientists say man-made warming is driving extreme weather. Sad!

With Irma and Harvey devastation, Tax Cuts and Tax Reform is needed more than ever before. Go Congress, go!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 13, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

With Irma and Harvey devastation, Tax Cuts and Tax Reform is needed more than ever before. Go Congress, go! I wish we had a little smiley face thing that connoted sarcasm, because a few dimwits might think this is serious.

The "deplorables" came back to haunt Hillary.They expressed their feelings loud and clear. She spent big money but, in the end, had no game!

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) September 14, 2017

@realDonaldTrump + 140 more characters

The “deplorables” came back to haunt Hillary.They expressed their feelings loud and clear. She spent big money but, in the end, had no game! I won by negative 3 million votes. Our electoral system is badly broken. Must fix!
