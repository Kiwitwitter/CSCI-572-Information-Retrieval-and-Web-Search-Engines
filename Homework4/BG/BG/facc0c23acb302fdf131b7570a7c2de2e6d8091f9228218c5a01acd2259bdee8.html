FALL FASHION | MAGAZINE

Three fall fashion trends to perk up your wardrobe

Plus a cheat sheet to the colors, accessories, fabrics, silhouettes, and more that are en vogue this season.

photographs except slip dress getty images

At left, from Dolce & Gabbana’s fall show in Milan; center, from left: Vogue’s Anna Wintour and actress Blake Lively at New York’s Fall Fashion Week; right, Abby slip dress at Ouimillie.

By Marni Elyse Katz   September 19, 2017

This season’s fashion inspirations range from the good old American West to present-day politics. Feminine but a little dark, plush with a shot of color, and space-age shiny are just a few of the fresh statements you can choose from. Scan this, then shop like a genius.

TREND: DARK FLORALS

Why we love it: Simultaneously edgy and feminine

Advertisement

Who showed it: Dolce & Gabbana, Gucci, Prabal Gurung, Simone Rocha

Who’s wearing it: Kate Middleton, Michelle Obama, Anna Wintour, Blake Lively

How to wear it: Paired with a red, pointy-toed bootie

Find it locally: Crush, Flock, Nomad

Buy it now: Abby slip dress by Baum und Pferdgarten, $215 at Ouimillie, 133 Charles Street, Boston, 617-982-7962, ouimillie.com

photos except LIT Boutique top from getty images

Left, from the Tome fall show in New York; center, top at LIT Boutique; left, from the Altuzarra fall show in New York.

TREND: MARIGOLD VELVET

Advertisement

Why we love it: Transitions easily from day to night

Who showed it: Altuzarra, Creatures of Comfort, Marc Jacobs, Tome

Who’s wearing it: Kendall Jenner, Kyra Sedgwick, Kelly Rowland, Emily Ratajkowski

How to wear it: Dress down with strong black accents; after hours add statement jewelry

Find it locally: Dress, LuxCouture, Portobello Road

Buy it now: Top by Blank NYC, $88 at LIT Boutique, 236 Hanover Street, Boston, 617-391-0086, and 223 Newbury Street, Boston, 617-421-8637; litboutique.com

all photos except French Lessons top from getty images

Left, from the Michael Kors Collection fall show in New York; second from left, from the Christopher Kane fall show in London; second from right, top at French Lessons.

TREND: SPACE-AGE SILVER

Why we love it: It’s fun and doesn’t have to be flashy

Who showed it: Balenciaga, Chanel, Christopher Kane, Michael Kors

Who’s wearing it: Cara Delevingne, Katy Perry, Solange Knowles, Penelope Cruz

How to wear it: Monochromatically with minimal accessories and a touch of white or black

Find it locally: All Too Human, dresscode, kloTH

Buy it now: Met top by Rebecca Taylor, $295 at French Lessons, 180B Linden Street, Wellesley, 781-235-9090, and other locations
